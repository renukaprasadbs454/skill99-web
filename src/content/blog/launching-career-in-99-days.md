Mastering a skill quickly takes more than watching tutorials. At Skill99, we break down the 99‑day journey into weekly project milestones, mentor office hours, and placement rituals that keep momentum without burning out.

Every cohort begins with a success contract, mapping what is expected to what each mentor will help you deliver—career documents, technical projects, and interview preparedness.
